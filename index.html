Stories told through

I'm Gowtham — a freelance photographer based in the heart of the city, with a deep passion for visual storytelling. For over eight years, I've been capturing the beauty in both extraordinary moments and quiet, everyday scenes.

My work spans portraits, landscapes, editorial, and street photography. I believe every frame has a story waiting to be told — my job is to find it and preserve it with intention and honesty.
